Annual Leave Policy

Effective Date: 15-12-2023

1. Introduction

At LM solutions, we understand the importance of work-life balance and recognize the need for employees to take time off to recharge, attend to personal matters, or handle unforeseen situations. This Leave Policy outlines the guidelines and procedures for requesting and managing leaves. It is essential that all employees understand and adhere to these policies to ensure a smooth and fair leave management process.

2. Types of Leaves

We offer various types of leaves to cater to the different needs of our employees. These include:

  • 2.1. Annual Leave (AL)
  • 2.2. Sick Leave (SL)
  • 2.3. Maternity and Paternity Leave
  • 2.4. Public Holidays

3. Leave Request Procedure

  1. 3.1. Submitting a Request
  2. 3.2. Approval Process
  3. 3.3. Notice Period

4. Leave Balances and Accrual

Leave balances and accrual rates vary depending on your employment contract and length of service. To check your leave balance, please refer to the Leave Management System or contact the HR department.

5. Leave Without Pay (LWOP)

In exceptional cases, employees may request Leave Without Pay. LWOP should be discussed with the HR department and approved by management.

6. Record Keeping

It is the responsibility of the HR department to maintain accurate leave records. Employees are encouraged to keep track of their own leave balances and notify HR of any discrepancies.

7. Abuse of Leave Policy

Any abuse of the leave policy, including fraudulent leave requests or patterns of unapproved absence, may result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

8. Conclusion

LM solutions is committed to supporting its employees in maintaining a healthy work-life balance. This Leave Policy is designed to ensure that leave is managed fairly and transparently for all employees. It is the responsibility of every employee to familiarize themselves with this policy and adhere to it.

Please feel free to contact the HR department for any questions or concerns regarding your leave.

LM solutions Leave Policy is subject to change, and any updates will be communicated to all employees through official channels.
